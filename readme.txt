Meowfia
Team Yahoo: Avery Holsinger, Jathin Banala, Nick Greco, Mason Niemas

Instructions:
- Press the right arrow key to progress through text.
- Left click on cards to play them in battle, or select them in between battles.
- Each card has an energy cost displayed in top left, and a description of its effect on the bottom half.
- Cards require and use up the specified energy when played.
- Your turn ends when you have no more playable cards (do not have the energy to play, or have no cards in your hand).
- You may have cards that raise or lower stats, and enemies may have abilities that do the same. You can see this by the display of your health and the enemies health (near the center of the screen).
- On the start of your turn, you draw 5 cards. You always draw Punch and Guard cards. Others depend on cards you pick.
- The goal of each battle is to reduce your enemy to 0 health. If you get reduced to 0, you lose.
- Between battles, you may pick a card to add to your deck. 

Cheat Codes:
- Press ENTER to progress to the next room (only works on your turn when in battle).
